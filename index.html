Hello!

I am a fifth-year research scholar in the Laboratory for Computational Social Systems (LCS2) at IIIT-Delhi, where I am advised by Dr. Md. Shad Akhtar and Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty. My interests lie broadly at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, and Conversational AI with a focus on mental health counseling methods. In particular, my research aligns toward understanding mental health clients on digital counseling systems using NLP and Deep Learning. To know more, refer to my resume or drop me an email!
