Hello!

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at MBZUAI, UAE, working with Prof. Monojit Choudhury and Prof. Thamar Solorio. Previously, I did my PhD from IIIT Delhi, India, where I was advised by Prof. Shad Akhtar (FLaME Lab, IIIT Delhi) and Prof. Tanmoy Chakraborty (LCS2 Lab, IIT Delhi).

My research lies at the intersection of applied computational social science, psycholinguistics, and recently, sociolinguistics. My early works focused on using LLMs in the context of digital health, applying psycholinguistics to better understand social interaction behaviors and mental health in conversational systems. Recently, my interest has expanded to the study of culturality, exploring how LLMs can better understand and reflect cultural dynamics during social interactions.
